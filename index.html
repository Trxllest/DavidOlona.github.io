About Me...

"I am currently a Honours Health Studies student with a minor in Computing at the University of Waterloo, where I am also employed as a Research Assistant. With early aspirations to become a doctor, things quickly began to change when I took a computer science course at the university. The thrill of solving problems and the potential to create anything I wanted attracted me in ways medicine didn't. Coming from an unconventional background it was not easy but I hope to document my progress using this website."
